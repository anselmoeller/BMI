BMI Calculator

BMI is the acronym for Body Mass Index

BMI is a parameter adopted by the World Health Organization to calculate the ideal weight of each person.

The index is calculated as follows:

the patient's weight is divided by their height squared.

An individual is said to have normal weight when the BMI result is between 18.5 and 24.9.
